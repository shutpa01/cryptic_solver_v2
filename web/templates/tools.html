Crossword Solving Tools

Stuck on a clue? These tools search over 500,000 real crossword answers from the Telegraph, Times, Guardian, Independent, and Daily Mail. No dictionary fluff, no made-up words — just answers that have actually appeared in published puzzles.

🔄
Anagram Solver
Got jumbled letters? I'll unscramble them. Use dashes for multi-word answers.
🔍
Pattern Matcher
Know some letters? Type what you've got, question marks for the rest. I'll find what fits.
📚
Synonym Finder
What does a word mean in crossword-land? Find every synonym, abbreviation, and definition the setters use.

Here's what makes these different. If I recognise the puzzle you're working on, I'll offer you the whole thing — all the clues with definitions, wordplay types, and step-by-step explanations. And on the puzzle page, you can click any word in any clue to feed it straight into the solver. No typing, no copying. Just click.
