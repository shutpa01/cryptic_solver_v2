## 6a: ACES — charade + last letter
**"Mean waitress finally serves?" (4)**

This is a nice short clue — just four words, and we know at least one is the definition.

Click on **finally**. See? It could be an indicator for **last letter**. Indicators usually sit next to their fodder — the word next to it is **waitress**, so maybe we need the last letter of waitress. That gives us **S**.

We need 3 more letters for a 4-letter answer. And look — we already have a crossing letter: **C** in the second position. Click on **mean** and look at the synonyms. Which 3-letter word starting with something, then C, could go with S to mean **serves**?

**ACE** — and ACES is what happens when a tennis player delivers a serve the opponent can't return!

Click ACE to put it in the answer box, then type S at the end. Hit **Check** — correct! **Add to grid** and watch the crossing letters update.

Now click the **Explanation** button — just as we thought: ACE (mean) + S (last letter of waitress) = ACES. The wordplay type is a **charade** — pieces joined together.

## 9a: DOGMA — charade + reversal
**"Icon returned mother's unquestioning faith" (5)**

Crossings: D_G_A — we already know three letters!

Click on **returned** — it's flagged as a **reversal indicator**. Something is being reversed. What's next to it? **Icon**. What's a 3-letter word for icon that reversed gives us D-O-G? GOD reversed = DOG!

Now we need two more letters. Click **mother's** — see the abbreviation **MA**? DOG + MA = DOGMA. And "unquestioning faith" is the definition — that's exactly what dogma means.

Type DOGMA, Check, Add to grid.

## 15a: BUNDLE — charade + deletion

## 4d: SHOCKING — charade + deletion

## 22d: NARKY — hidden reversed
**"Crikey! Krankies not all revolting and irritable" (5)**

Crossings: __R_Y

Click **revolting** — it's a **reversal indicator**. And "not all" suggests a **hidden word** — but reversed!

Look at "Crikey Krankies" backwards: ...SEIKNAR YEKIRC... Can you spot NARKY hiding in YEIKNAR? Actually: crikEY KRANkies reversed contains Y-K-R-A-N = NARKY!

"Irritable" is the definition. This is a **hidden reversed** clue — the answer is spelled backwards across the clue words.
